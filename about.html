About Our Hospital

Trusted Healthcare With Compassionate Patient Care

Sri Vinayaka Hospital was established 25+ years ago with a mission to provide high-quality medical care at affordable costs for families across Bengaluru.

We provide patient-focused healthcare services with experienced doctors, modern facilities, emergency support, diagnostics, inpatient care and advanced surgical treatment support.

Our hospital is committed to delivering safe, transparent and compassionate care so every patient receives the right guidance, timely treatment and a comfortable healthcare experience.

Our Story

25+ Years of Trusted Healthcare

Sri Vinayaka Hospital was founded with the goal of making quality healthcare accessible and affordable for families in Bengaluru.

Over the years, the hospital has continued to serve patients with general medicine, women care, pediatrics, emergency services, diagnostics, surgeries, inpatient care and specialist consultations.

Our journey continues with the same purpose: to provide compassionate, ethical and patient-focused healthcare for every individual who trusts us with their care.
