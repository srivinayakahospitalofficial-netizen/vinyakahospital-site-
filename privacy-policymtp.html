Privacy Policy

Sri Vinayaka Hospital – Women & Multispeciality Hospital, Bengaluru

Introduction

Sri Vinayaka Hospital respects the privacy and dignity of every patient. We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal and medical information while providing quality healthcare services.

This Privacy Policy explains how information collected through our website, appointment forms, telephone enquiries, WhatsApp communication, and in-person consultations is handled.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or visit our hospital:

  • Patient Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address (if provided)
  • Age and Gender
  • Medical History shared during consultation
  • Appointment Details
  • Diagnostic Reports
  • Treatment Information
  • Hospital Visit Details

Purpose of Information Collection

Your information is collected only for legitimate healthcare purposes, including:

  • Booking appointments
  • Providing medical consultation
  • Diagnosis and treatment planning
  • Follow-up care
  • Patient communication
  • Hospital administration
  • Billing and medical records
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

Confidential Women's Healthcare

Sri Vinayaka Hospital understands that women's healthcare requires complete privacy, dignity and respect.

All consultations are conducted confidentially by qualified healthcare professionals. Patient information is accessed only by authorized medical personnel involved in providing healthcare services.

We maintain strict confidentiality regarding consultations, investigations, diagnosis, treatment and medical records unless disclosure is required under applicable law.

Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP) Privacy

For patients seeking consultation related to Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP), Sri Vinayaka Hospital follows applicable legal requirements and professional medical standards.

Every enquiry, consultation, investigation and treatment is handled with complete confidentiality and sensitivity.

Personal information shared through our website, telephone calls, WhatsApp messages or hospital visits is used solely for providing healthcare services and is not disclosed to unauthorized persons.

Only registered medical practitioners and authorized healthcare staff directly involved in patient care have access to relevant medical information.

Privacy Commitment

Patient confidentiality is one of our highest priorities. We are committed to maintaining privacy throughout consultation, treatment and follow-up care in accordance with applicable laws and accepted medical practice.

Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect patient information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or misuse.

  • Secure hospital records
  • Restricted staff access
  • Password protected systems
  • Controlled document handling
  • Secure storage of patient information

Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve website performance, user experience and service quality. These technologies do not collect confidential medical information without your knowledge.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links or integrations such as Google Maps, WhatsApp or Meta services. These third-party platforms have their own privacy policies and terms of use.

Your Rights

  • Request information regarding your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request updates to your contact information.
  • Discuss concerns regarding privacy with hospital administration.

Policy Updates

Sri Vinayaka Hospital reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy whenever necessary to comply with legal, regulatory or operational requirements. Updated policies will be published on this page.
