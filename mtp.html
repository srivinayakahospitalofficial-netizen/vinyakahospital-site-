What is Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP)?

Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP) is a medically supervised treatment provided by qualified gynecologists based on individual health conditions and applicable medical guidelines.

Every patient receives personalized evaluation, consultation, privacy-focused care, and guidance throughout the treatment and recovery process.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MTP confidential?
Yes. Patient privacy and confidentiality are maintained throughout consultation, evaluation, treatment, and follow-up care. Your medical information is handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with applicable laws and professional standards.
Can I consult a gynecologist before making a decision?
Yes. Consultation with a qualified gynecologist is the first step to understand your medical condition, available options, and receive appropriate guidance based on your individual health needs.
Is follow-up support available?
Yes. Recovery guidance and follow-up support are provided to help ensure patient comfort, well-being, and appropriate medical care after treatment.
Who is eligible for MTP treatment?
Eligibility for Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP) is determined only after a medical evaluation by a qualified gynecologist. The doctor will assess your health condition and advise you according to applicable medical guidelines and legal requirements.
Is MTP performed as per Government norms?
Yes. Sri Vinayaka Hospital provides MTP services in accordance with the Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP) Act, applicable Government of India regulations, and accepted medical standards. Every case is evaluated individually by qualified medical professionals before treatment.
